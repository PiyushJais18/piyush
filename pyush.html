1- POLITICAL NEWS

Political news is a branch of journalism focused on reporting and analyzing current events, issues, and individuals within the political sphere. It tracks political activities, government actions, and power dynamics at local, national, and international levels to keep the public informed

2- BUISNEES NEWS

Business news consists of reports and analyses of economic, financial, and commercial activities, including news about companies, markets, investments, and economic trends. It helps people make informed financial decisions, promotes transparency in business, and tracks how economic changes affect individuals, companies, and the broader economy.

3-FORIGN NEWS

International news refers to news about events that occur outside of a specific country or region and have global significance. It covers political, economic, social, and cultural developments that impact multiple countries and is often gathered by foreign correspondents or through distance communication technologies.

4-CULTURAL NEWS

"Cultural news" refers to reporting on the arts, creative works, and broader societal values, customs, and behaviors of a group of people. It includes coverage of traditional arts like literature and music, as well as popular culture like film and television, and explores how these elements are influenced by and shape a society. Essentially, it's a form of journalism that looks at news through a cultural lens, examining the shared knowledge, creative output, and ways of living of a community.

5-FASHION NEWS

Fashion news covers developments in the clothing, footwear, accessories, and cosmetics industries, including new trends, designer collections, business deals, and industry events. It encompasses a wide range of topics, from the latest styles and celebrity influences to business and sustainability issues within the fashion world.

6-SPORTS NEWS

Sports news means information and updates about sporting events, teams, athletes, and the industry itself. It includes coverage of scores, schedules, player injuries, trades, and other relevant news, presented through various forms like game stories, feature articles, and analysis.
